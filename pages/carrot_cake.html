Carrot Cake Recipe!

Description

A cake made out of carrot!

Ingredients

  1. 2 Cups of All-purpose flour
  2. 2 teaspoons of baking soda
  3. 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  4. 1.5 teaspoon of ground cinnamon
  5. 1.25 cups of canola oil
  6. 1 cup of sugar
  7. 1 cup of brown sugar
  8. 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  9. 4 large eggs
  10. 3 cups of peeled carrots
  11. 1 cup of coarsly chopped pecans
  12. 1/2 cup of raisins

For the frosting

  1. 8 ounces of cream cheese
  2. 1.25 cups of powdered sugar
  3. 1/3 cup of heavy whipping cream
  4. 1/2 cup of coarsly chopped pecans
