Lasagne Recipe!

Description

Easy Lasagne that you can prepare ahead of time and save in the freezer, uncooked, for when you need it during the week

Ingredients

  • 2tbsp olive oil
  • 750g of lean beef
  • 90g pack of proscuitto
  • tomato sauce
  • 200ml hot beef stock
  • Grated nutmeg
  • 300g pack of lasagne sheets
  • white sauce
  • 125g of mozzarella, torn into thin strips

Steps

  1. Heat 2tbsp in a frying pan
  2. Cut those bones down
  3. Chop 4 slces of prosciutto. Stir meat through meetings
  4. Pour over 800g passata or half our basic tomato sauce recipe and 200ml hot beef stock. Add a little grated nutmeg, then season.
  5. Bring to a boil, then simmer for 30mins until sauce looks rich
  6. Heat oven to 180C
  7. Spoon 1/3 of the meat sauce into the dish, cover with some fresh lasagne sheets from a 300g pack. Drizle over 130g ready-made / homemade white sauce
  8. Repeat until you have 3 layers of pasta. Cover with the remaining 390g white sauce. Make sure you can't see any pasta poking out
  9. Scatter 125g of torn mozzarella over the top
  10. Arrange the rest of the prosciutto on top. Bake for 45 min until the top is bubbling and lightly browned
